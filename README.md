## 🧭 What This App Does

YouTube-to-Cloud-Downloader helps you save YouTube videos and audio files from a simple web-style interface. You can keep files on your Google Drive or download them to your PC.

It uses yt-dlp to fetch media in common formats like MP4 and MP3. The app works through Google Colab, so you do not need to write code to use it.

## ✅ Main Features

- Download YouTube videos in MP4
- Extract audio in MP3
- Save files to Google Drive
- Save files to your local PC
- Pick high-quality video up to 4K
- Use a simple, code-free interface
- Handle single videos and audio-only downloads
- Keep your files organized for later use

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### The download does not start
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure the YouTube link is correct
- Try another video

### The app cannot access Google Drive
- Sign out and sign in again
- Allow all Drive access prompts
- Refresh the browser page

### The file is too large
- Pick a lower video quality
- Use MP3 instead of MP4
- Save to Google Drive if your PC space is low

### The video has no sound
- Pick a different format
- Try a standard MP4 option
- Re-run the download

### The browser closes the page
- Keep the tab open until the job ends
- Do not close the Colab page during the download
